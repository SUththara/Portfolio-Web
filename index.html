Research Project

An Emotion-Based Activity Suggestion System for Tourists (Individual Component)

This research presents an innovative Emotion-Based Activity Suggestion System leveraging mobile phone cameras to gauge user emotions and offer tailored activity recommendations, aiming to enhance satisfaction and engagement. Using facial recognition and deep learning, it identifies emotions, associating them with diverse activities. Tests showcased over 90% accuracy in emotion detection, underscoring the system's potential. To enhance performance, the study recommends stringent privacy measures, ethical considerations, and continual algorithm refinement through user feedback for a more robust recommendation engine.
